About

Welcome to Mini Job Portal — a simple place to browse a few openings and submit your application. We review applications and contact shortlisted candidates by email.

How it works:

  • Check the job list below.
  • Choose the job you want in the form.
  • Fill in your details and a short message.
  • Click “Submit Application”.

Need help? Email us at jobs@minijobportal.example. We respect your privacy and only use your info for hiring.
